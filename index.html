Our Story

Who We Are

Hidden Treasure Primary School Maya was founded on a deeply held belief — that every child carries a unique, God-given treasure waiting to be discovered and developed. Nestled in the Maya community of Wakiso District, our school has grown into a trusted institution known for discipline, academic excellence, and a sincere love for every pupil in our care.


We are a mixed day and boarding nursery and primary school, licensed and registered by the Ministry of Education and Sports of Uganda, and an approved examination centre under the Uganda National Examinations Board (UNEB).


Our school embraces a child-centred philosophy that promotes creative thinking, lifelong learning, and strong Christian values. Through partnerships with parents and the community, we strive to make every academic dream a reality.

Our Curriculum

Nursery through Primary Seven — a UNEB-aligned education enriched with faith and co-curricular excellence

Hidden Treasure Primary School follows the Uganda National Curriculum as presented by UNEB, structured across three cycles: Early Childhood, Lower Primary, and Upper Primary — enriched with Christian values, junior literature, etiquette lessons, and a wide co-curricular programme.

Nursery Section

Our nursery section lays the critical foundation for every child's learning journey in a warm, play-based, faith-filled environment.

Our Lower Primary Approach

We diagnose each child's learning needs early. Our teachers provide targeted support and regular individual feedback, ensuring no child is left behind at this critical stage.

  • Small class sizes for close attention
  • Regular reading assessments
  • Learning difficulty identification
  • Parental progress updates

Join Our Family

How to Apply

We welcome applications for all classes — Nursery through Primary Seven. Enrolment is open every term. Contact us early — spaces are limited!

1

Visit & Inquire

Call or visit the school to learn about available spaces, the fee structure, and what makes Hidden Treasure special.

2

Submit Application

Collect the admission form from our office, fill it in, attach the required documents, and submit to our registrar.

3

Assessment

Your child attends a brief, friendly assessment to help us place them in the most appropriate class for their level.

4

Enrolment

Pay fees, collect the uniform list and booklist, and officially welcome your child to the Hidden Treasure family!

Required Documents

  • Child's birth certificate (photocopy)
  • Previous school report card (for transfers)
  • 2 passport-size photographs
  • Parent/guardian National ID (copy)
  • Completed admission form
  • Immunisation card (where applicable)

School Terms 2025

  • Term 1 — February to May
  • Term 2 — June to August
  • Term 3 — September to December
  • Enrolment open for all three terms
  • Early registration is strongly recommended
