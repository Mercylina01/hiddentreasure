Our Story

Who We Are

Hidden Treasure Children's Foundation was born from a deeply held belief — that every child carries a unique, God-given treasure waiting to be discovered and developed. What began as a single school in Maya, Wakiso District has grown into a Foundation with two branches, serving hundreds of children across Wakiso and Mpigi districts.


We are a mixed day and boarding nursery and primary school group, licensed and registered by the Ministry of Education and Sports of Uganda, and an approved examination centre under the Uganda National Examinations Board (UNEB).


Our schools embrace a child-centred philosophy that promotes creative thinking, lifelong learning, and strong Christian values. Through partnerships with parents and the community, we strive to make every academic dream a reality.

How to Apply

We welcome applications for all classes — Nursery through Primary Seven — at both our Maya and Kamengo branches. Enrolment is open every term. Contact us early — spaces are limited!

1

Choose Your Branch

Decide whether your child will attend our Maya (Wakiso) or Kamengo (Mpigi) branch, then call or visit to confirm available spaces.

2

Submit Application

Collect the admission form from the school office, fill it in, attach the required documents, and submit to our registrar.

3

Assessment

Your child attends a brief, friendly assessment to help us place them in the most appropriate class for their level.

4

Enrolment

Pay fees, collect the uniform list and booklist, and officially welcome your child to the Hidden Treasure family!

Required Documents

  • Child's birth certificate (photocopy)
  • Previous school report card (for transfers)
  • 2 passport-size photographs
  • Parent/guardian National ID (copy)
  • Completed admission form
  • Immunisation card (where applicable)

School Terms 2025

  • Term 1 — February to May
  • Term 2 — June to August
  • Term 3 — September to December
  • Enrolment open for all three terms
  • Early registration strongly recommended
